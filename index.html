About Me:

I'm a young female programmer from Germany who makes some fun idle games and other projects in her free time, for free! I enjoy updating my projects, expanding them, following the bigger vision, releasing many updates, but they always contain interesting content. Quantity and quality! I love interacting with the players, giving advice, seeing them discuss my games and playing the games myself.
I am also an artist (image editing, pixel art, game sprites and more). I also really like wikis and playing games. Nowadays I am a calm and friendly person, I am almost never mad, angry or sad. The best way to contact me is via Discord.

Schrottii's Games


Schrottii's Games is my Discord server, it is dedicated to my games and other work! You have at least one channel for each game here and people to discuss them with. I am, of course, very active in here. It's well moderated and safe.

Toast


Toast is my Discord server that has been active since 2018, it offers a small weird community for talking about games, shitposting and having fun. It used to be the main place of discussion for most of my games, but the new server has taken that function. I am active in the server. Be warned, the members are not afraid to go pretty far with jokes and potentially post offensive content.

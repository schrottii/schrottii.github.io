About Me:

I'm a young female programmer from Germany who makes some fun games and other projects in her free time, for free! I enjoy updating my projects, expanding them, following the bigger vision, releasing many updates, but they always contain interesting content. Quantity and quality! I like interacting with the players, giving advice, seeing them discuss my games and playing the games myself.
I am also an artist (image editing, pixel art, game sprites and more). I also really like wikis and playing games. I have been through a lot but will keep providing content and keep going. The best way to contact me is via Discord.

Schrottii's Games


Schrottii's Games is my Discord server, dedicated to these games and other projects! There is at least one channel for every game here and people to discuss them with. I am, of course, very active in here. It's well moderated and safe, but more importantly, the best source for news and work-in-progress insights of upcoming content!

Toast


Toast is my community server that has been active since 2018, it offers a small weird community for talking about other games, shitposting and having fun. It used to be the main place of discussion for most of my games, but the new server has taken that function. I am active in the server. Be warned, the members are not afraid to go pretty far with jokes and post potentially offensive content.

Settings

Direct Play - adds buttons to quickly play a game

Local Dir - available if direct play is set to local. Use this if you have this website and the games saved on your device and prefer playing them locally. Use this to enter the path where they are saved. Games need to follow the repo names (e. g. "shgabb-clicker")

Note: This website, just like my games, doesn't save or care about your personal data, only your settings/progress are saved.
